# Privacy Policy — BrainEx

**Last updated: 2026-06-08**

BrainEx ("the app") does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What the app does

- Reads Windows system notifications locally on your device to display task cards.
- Uses Win32 APIs to activate and bring other application windows to the foreground.

## What the app does NOT do

- The app does **not** collect any personal information.
- The app does **not** send any data to external servers or third parties.
- The app does **not** track usage, analytics, or telemetry.
- The app does **not** require an account or login.

## Data storage

All information processed by the app (notification content, task state) exists only in memory during the app's runtime and is never persisted to disk or transmitted over any network.
